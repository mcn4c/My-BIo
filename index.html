How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Get two pieces of sandwich bread
  2. Spread mayonnaise on one piece of bread
  3. Place three pieces of ham atop the mayonnaise
  4. Place a piece of cheddar cheese atop the ham
  5. Put the other piece of bread atop the cheddar cheese and cut sandwich in half
